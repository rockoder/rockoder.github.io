## Overview

Zalando is Europe's leading online platform for fashion and lifestyle, serving over 50 million active customers across 25 markets. As an Engineering Manager, I lead teams responsible for building and maintaining critical platform services.

## Role & Responsibilities

### People Leadership
- Hiring and onboarding engineers
- Career development and mentorship
- Performance management
- Building inclusive team culture

### Technical Direction
- Architectural decisions and technical strategy
- Code review and engineering standards
- Production reliability and incident management
- Cross-team collaboration

### Delivery
- Sprint planning and execution
- Stakeholder management
- Risk assessment and mitigation
- Continuous improvement

## Key Focus Areas

### Team Building
Building diverse, high-performing teams through structured hiring processes, clear expectations, and continuous feedback.

### Technical Excellence
Maintaining high engineering standards while enabling rapid delivery through:
- Code review practices
- Testing strategies
- Documentation standards
- On-call processes

### Engineering Culture
Fostering an environment where engineers can do their best work:
- Psychological safety
- Knowledge sharing
- Technical growth opportunities
- Work-life balance

## Challenges & Learnings

### Scaling Teams
Growing teams while maintaining culture and quality requires intentional effort in:
- Onboarding processes
- Communication patterns
- Decision-making frameworks

### Balancing Priorities
Managing the tension between:
- Feature delivery and technical debt
- Individual growth and team needs
- Short-term wins and long-term vision

## Results

- Successfully scaled engineering teams
- Improved delivery velocity and predictability
- Established sustainable engineering practices
- Mentored engineers toward career growth

## Lessons Learned

1. **People first** - Technical challenges are usually people challenges in disguise
2. **Context matters** - Leadership approaches must adapt to team, project, and organizational context
3. **Trust takes time** - Building trust requires consistent actions over time
4. **Continuous learning** - The best leaders are perpetual students
